WHO WE ARE

We have the most qualified instructors in your hometown.

KM Foundation was formed in the year of 2010 and has evolved to become one of the most trusted educational organizations in India. Our organization is headed by leading professionals from various spheres and has branched into all districts of Kerala. We also have our sister concern in most of the overseas destinations. We get students admitted only in colleges with valid affiliations like AICTE, Indian medical council, Indian nursing council, state government and university approved colleges. We guide you to world class colleges and universities in Kerala, Karnataka, Tamilnadu, Pondicherry and Maharashtra, which offer undergraduate, post graduate and diploma courses, as well as doctoral research in wide ranging disciplines like engineering, medicine, Nano medical sciences, Ayurveda, management education, mass communication etc…
